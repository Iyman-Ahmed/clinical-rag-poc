# Medicare Telehealth Policy — Calendar Year 2024

## Covered services and originating site (CY2024)
Under the telehealth flexibilities extended through 2024, Medicare beneficiaries
could receive a broad set of telehealth services in their home, and the
geographic originating-site restrictions that normally limit telehealth to
approved rural sites were waived.

## Audio-only services (CY2024)
Audio-only communication was permitted for a defined set of services, including
behavioral health and selected evaluation and management services, when the
patient could not use, or did not consent to, video technology.

## Billing convention
Telehealth claims were billed with the appropriate CPT/HCPCS code and a telehealth
Place of Service code (10 = telehealth in the patient's home; 02 = telehealth
other than the home). Modifier 95 indicated a synchronous audio-video service.

## Frequency limitations
During 2024, frequency limitations for certain subsequent inpatient and nursing
facility visits and critical care consultations furnished via telehealth were
suspended under the active flexibilities.
