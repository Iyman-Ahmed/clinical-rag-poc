# Medicare Telehealth Policy — Calendar Year 2025

> SOURCE: CMS CY2025 Physician Fee Schedule final rule and CMS telehealth
> guidance (MLN901705, "Telehealth & Remote Monitoring");
> https://www.cms.gov/medicare/coverage/telehealth
> Retrieved 2026-06-29. Summarized for demonstration; verify against CMS source.

## Audio-only telehealth (CY2025)
For CY2025, CMS finalized that an interactive telecommunications system may
include two-way, real-time, audio-only communication technology for any
telehealth service furnished to a patient in their home, IF the distant-site
practitioner is technically capable of using an audio-video system but the
patient is not capable of, or does not consent to, using video technology.

## Billing convention
Audio-only services are billed using the applicable E/M code with **modifier 93**
(audio-only synchronous telemedicine), or **modifier FQ** for Rural Health Clinics
and Federally Qualified Health Centers, plus the applicable Place of Service code.
Modifier 95 denotes a synchronous audio-video telemedicine service.

## Frequency limitations
Frequency limitations for subsequent inpatient visits, subsequent nursing
facility visits, and critical care consultations furnished via telehealth remain
suspended through December 31, 2025.

## Extension of flexibilities
The Consolidated Appropriations Act, 2026 extended a number of Medicare telehealth
flexibilities through December 31, 2027.

## Originating site
Behavioral and mental health services may continue to be furnished with the
patient's home as the originating site. Certain non-behavioral telehealth
services remain subject to statutory originating-site and geographic rules absent
an active flexibility.
